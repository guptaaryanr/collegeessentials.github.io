Bed, Bath, and Beyond

  • Comforter and/or quilts (one made of soft, not synthetic material would be the best)
  • Throw blanket
  • Bed sheets (depending on the mattress in your room but it's most likely to be twin XL)
  • Pillows
  • Pillowcases
  • Mattress pad
  • Reading pillow
  • Alarm clock
  • Tissue
  • Paper towels
  • Under bed storage
  • Towels: bath, washcloths and hand towels
  • Shower shoes (because community showers)
  • Shower bucket/basket/caddy to carry items
  • Extra pillow(s) if you will lounge/study on the bed

  • Deodorant
  • Hair grooming tools (combs, hair dryer, brush)
  • Toothbrush and toothpaste
  • Lotion/skin cosmetics

  • Travel soap container
  • Dental floss
  • Eye drops
  • Q-tips
  • Mouthwash
  • Nail clippers
  • Loofah (please scrub yourself, you animals)
  • Cotton swabs
  • Shaving kit

  • Prescription medicines and copies of each prescription
  • Bathroom cleaning products (for attached or personal bathrooms)
  • Shower mat
  • Air Freshener
  • Scale
  • Thermometer
  • Heating pad
  • Air purifier
