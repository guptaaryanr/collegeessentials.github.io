The usual essentials for a freshman student at a college seem obvious to many people, but it's hard to think of everything. Personal items from home will aid in the transition and are easy enough to remember, but you'll need more than bedding and a laptop to get through the year.

This college essentials website is to help you with all the essentials you might need for college. The Essentials page lists all the items needed in neat categories for you to make sense of it and the List page is a helpful To-Do type tool to help you keep track of the items you may need to get and the ones you've already gotten
